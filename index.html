Supporting Air Defense Operations

Drone attacks continue to target civilian areas across Ukraine, requiring ongoing support for defense operations. Volunteer units work to protect communities and critical infrastructure.

Ukraine Shield provides support to volunteer defense units, including radar equipment, thermal imaging systems, fuel for mobile units, and training for drone detection teams.

Our volunteers work to monitor and respond to threats, helping protect communities. Your donations help fund equipment and supplies for these operations.
